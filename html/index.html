Dota 2 : The International

The International Is a Dota 2 tournament game that is held directly from Valve that produces and publishes the game itself. Started the first competition at the opening event in Germany in 2011, with the prize money originally worth up to US $ 1 million. But after continuing until year 9 (2019) Valve has sold products related to The International With 25% of the money sold, will be the total prize money in the competition Making each year more and more value And the race is harder every year as well
